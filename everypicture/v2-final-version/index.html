A's baseball game

During my junior year of high school, I attended my first baseball game at the A’s with my youth group! I never understood how baseball works but it was a really exciting experience just being with my friends and mentor.

Dorm Name tag

I joined the STEP program during my first summer before my Freshman year. This is where I meet most of the first generation college students. I got paired with my amazing roommate, Avaa who I got close with throughout the year!

Yosemite Trip

This was my first camping trip in the snow when I was in 5th grade! As an immigrant, I never get to experience camping when I was in China. The experience taught me survival skills and making lasting memories from my elementary school.

Inside Out movie with Mom

Growing up in China, I never got to experience the luxury of watching movies in a theatre. Coming to the US, I get to experience many things with my mom, such as going to the theatre. Looking back, the physical movie ticket gives me nostalgia as they have become digital now.

Minions movie with Mom

When I first saw the Minions movie, I loved how adorable the animations are. Back when I was in Elementary school, there was less flexibility to watch it with friends and I’ve always experienced new things with my mom. I always cherish all the moments that I have with her!
